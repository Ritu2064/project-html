HYBRID COMPUTER

Hybrid computers are computers that exhibit features of analog computers and digital computers. The digital component normally serves as the controller and provides logical and numerical operations, while the analog component often serves as a solver of differential equations and other mathematically complex problems.



    Characteristics of Hybrid computer
  1. It is the combination of the qualities of analog as well as digital computers.
  2. It can process both continuous-discrete dates.
  3. It has the capability to convert one type of data into another.
  4. It is a special-purpose Computer.

    Benefits of hybrid computer
  1. Better support for a remote workforce.
  2. Improved scalability and control.
  3. Increased agility and innovation.
  4. Improved security and risk management.

    Some examples of hybrid computer
  1. Ultrasound Machine.
  2. Electrocardiogram Machine.
  3. Monitoring Machine.
  4. Research and Production Industries.
  5. Forensic.
